Maroulosalata

Are you someone that hates salads but wants to try eating healthy? Or someone who loves them but wants to try a different technique? Then you should try this yummy Greek Maroulosalata...

Ingredients:

  • romaine lettuce
  • scallions
  • dill
  • creamy feta
    • olive oil
  • olives(optional)
  • lemon juice
  • salt

Recipe:

  • Toss chopped: romaine, scallions, dill and feta into a bowl.
  • Mix some olive oil with lemon juice and salt.
  • Add olives if wanted.
  • Mix gently with your hands.
  • Enjoy!

Fact about Maroulosalata:

Maroulosalata is a Greek lettuce salad - in Greek, lettuce is marouli (μαρούλι), and salad is salata (σαλάτα), hence Maroulosalata which translates to Lettuce Salad. Traditionally this salad is made with crisp, thinly shredded lettuce, green onions and dill with a simple lemon and olive oil vinaigrette.
